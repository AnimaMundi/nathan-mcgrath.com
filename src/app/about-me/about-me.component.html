Who I am
I am a self-motivated and highly driven full stack web developer with 2 years professional experience in building highly functional cloud based applications. I have worked with startups in their earliest stages of development and enterprise companies with fully fledged software suites.
My experience across the board of cloud technologies, in combination with my drive, motivation and self starter attitude allows me to work extremely well on my own. But I thrive most of all in a team environment with other developers, where ideas can be born and grow from a collection of minds collaborating together for a common goal.
In a nutshell, my goal in life is to make computers behave intelligently. Whether that be in relation to cloud applications, games, gaming engines, physics & biology simulations, or artificial intelligence applications. Whatever it is that I'm doing, my single most obvious passion that carries through is to make software intuitive and work as we would expect it to.
